Receive your earnings within seven days after the end of every weekly period.

You'll receive commissions in USDT, paid directly to your wallet.

+ - Is there a limit to the number of referrals I can make?

Absolutely not – the sky's the limit!

Doing so would result in the forfeiture of any commissions earned from such actions.

+ - How do I track my referrals?

You're able to track your referrals in our referral dashboard.

+ - What if I refer someone, but they don't make a purchase right away?

If the person you've referred makes a purchase within 7 days of being referred, you will still be credited for that referral.
